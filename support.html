About LANC

LANC is a premium cycling system for managing bikes, builds, compatibility, Ride Plan decisions, gear, service history, costs and upgrade intelligence.

FAQ

How do I add a bike?

Open Garage, tap the add button and choose whether you want to start from the LANC Bike Catalog or enter the bike manually. You can edit the details later.

How do I connect Strava?

Open the Strava section in the app and tap Connect with Strava. LANC uses the official Strava login flow. You do not need to enter technical tokens manually.

How do I make a backup?

Open More, go to Backup and export your local data. Keep the exported file in a safe place if you want to restore it later.
